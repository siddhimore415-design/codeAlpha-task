# 📊 Sales Dashboard (Power BI)

## 📌 Project Overview

The **Sales Dashboard** is an interactive Business Intelligence solution developed using **Power BI** to analyze sales performance, profit trends, customer behavior, and product insights from **2020–2025**.

The dashboard transforms raw sales data into meaningful visual insights, enabling businesses to monitor performance, identify growth opportunities, and make data-driven decisions through dynamic reports and visualizations.

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

* Monitor overall sales and profit performance.
* Identify top-performing customers and product categories.
* Analyze regional sales distribution and trends.
* Track key business KPIs in real time.
* Support strategic decision-making through data visualization.
* Improve business understanding using interactive analytics.

---

## 🚀 Key Features

### 📈 Sales & Profit Analysis

* Analyze total sales, profit, and order performance.
* Compare profitability across product categories.
* Monitor business growth through visual KPIs.

### 👥 Customer Performance Insights

* Identify top customers contributing to revenue.
* Analyze customer purchasing patterns.
* Track customer impact on overall sales performance.

### 🛍 Product Category Analysis

* Evaluate category-wise profitability.
* Compare product demand across sub-categories.
* Identify high-performing and low-performing products.

### 🌍 Geographic Sales Visualization

* State-wise sales performance analysis.
* Interactive map visualizations for regional insights.
* Identify strong and underperforming markets.

### 📊 KPI Monitoring

* Real-time business performance indicators.
* Quick overview of sales, orders, profit, and quantity sold.
* Easy-to-understand dashboard metrics.

### 🎛 Interactive Dashboard Experience

* Dynamic slicers and filters.
* Drill-down analysis for deeper insights.
* User-friendly navigation and responsive visuals.
* Interactive charts, maps, and KPI cards.

---

## 📊 Dashboard Components

### 💰 Profit by Category

* Analyzes profitability across different product categories.
* Helps identify the most profitable business segments.

### 🍩 Quantity by Sub-Category

* Displays product demand using donut chart visualizations.
* Highlights top-selling sub-categories.

### 👤 Top Customers

* Identifies customers generating the highest sales revenue.
* Supports customer segmentation and retention strategies.

### 🗺 Sales by State

* Visualizes regional sales performance through interactive maps.
* Enables geographic trend analysis.

### 📌 KPI Cards

Provides a quick business overview through:

* Total Profit
* Total Orders
* Total Quantity Sold
* Category Performance
* Top Customers
* State-wise Sales

## 🎛 Dashboard Filters
