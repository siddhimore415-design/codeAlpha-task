🛒 E-Commerce Product Analysis Dashboard
📌 Project Overview

The E-Commerce Product Analysis Dashboard is an interactive Business Intelligence solution developed using Power BI to transform raw e-commerce data into actionable business insights. The dashboard enables users to explore sales performance, customer purchasing behavior, product ratings, and profit trends through dynamic visualizations and interactive filters.

By leveraging data analytics and visualization techniques, this project helps businesses identify profitable products, understand customer preferences, and make data-driven decisions to improve overall performance and growth.

🎯 Project Objectives
Analyze sales and profit performance across different regions.
Identify top-performing product categories and products.
Understand customer purchasing patterns and contribution to revenue.
Evaluate product ratings and customer satisfaction trends.
Discover state-wise business opportunities and profitability.
Support strategic business decisions using data-driven insights.

📈 Key Business Insights

✅ Punjab records the highest overall profit.

✅ Electronics is the top-performing category in both sales and profit.

✅ Most products receive customer ratings between 3 and 5 stars.

✅ A small group of top customers contributes significantly to total revenue.

✅ Kerala, Tamil Nadu, and Maharashtra demonstrate strong sales performance.

✅ Regional analysis reveals valuable opportunities for business expansion.
